Please download and install a suitable version based on your operation system. 
下载“腾讯会议”软件按照提示步骤安装完毕。
Follow the steps displayed, and complete the installation. ⼆、 打开并登录“腾讯会议”软件
Open and log in to Tencent Meeting
（1）双击“腾讯会议”软件按钮，打开腾讯会议程序。
Double click the Tencent Meeting to open the application. 
（2）SSO ⽅式登录腾讯会议
Choose the login method: SSO. 
选择“我知道企业域名”，填写 hkustgz 后点击登陆（如图）：
Click “I know enterprise domain”, and enter the domain name “hkustgz”
before you click “Log In” (as screenshots below). 点击登录按钮之后浏览器会自⾏跳转⾄“SSO 统⼀认证服务平台”页面，输⼊账号
及密码后登陆。
When you click “Log In”, you will go the the SSO authentication platform. Please 
enter the username and password of your GZ account. 登录成功之后 点击“启动应用”后腾讯会议软件即完成登陆。
After login, please allow to open Tencent Meeting.
